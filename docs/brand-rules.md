# Brand Rules

## Approved Brand Naming

- Full bridge brand: `Distilled Funding by Moonshine Capital`
- Short form: `Distilled Funding`
- Parent reference: `Moonshine Capital`

## Disallowed Naming

- `Capitol Accelerator`

Only mention the disallowed term when:

- documenting legacy cleanup
- quarantining a bad asset
- explicitly noting a term that must not ship

## Brand Intent

Use the bridge brand to make the operating relationship clear:

- `Distilled Funding` is the operating short form
- `Moonshine Capital` is the parent reference
- trust and disclosure copy should reinforce that relationship consistently

## Writing Rules

- Prefer factual, verifiable language
- Avoid inflated claims without proof
- Avoid contradictory company naming across pages
- Keep disclosure wording consistent once approved

## QA Checks

Before approving a page or template, confirm:

- brand name is correct in title, H1, body, metadata, and schema
- parent relationship is not contradicted
- disallowed legacy names are removed or explicitly quarantined
- disclosure language matches current approved wording
